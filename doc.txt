This project involves building an AI-powered lead generation system that identifies high-value luxury watch collectors from social media platforms such as Instagram and TikTok.



The system will automatically:



Scrape profiles from targeted accounts and relevant watch-related audiences



Collect public profile information such as bio, follower count, engagement, and recent posts



Use AI to classify profiles into collector, dealer, or non-relevant accounts



Identify likely watch brand interests (e.g., Rolex, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet, Richard Mille)



Store all structured data in Google Sheets for easy review and filtering
